About Revive IV.

Bringing hospital-grade therapies directly to you for optimal recovery and elevated wellness.

Our goal at Revive IV Hydration is to give you instantaneous relief and performance enhancement by delivering high-quality vitamins, hydration, and nutrients safely into your bloodstream.

Founded by registered nurses, our mission operates on professionalism, medical expertise, and an unwavering standard for care. Skip the ER and traditional clinics; we come to you wherever you are.

Our Commitment to Care

Safety and efficacy are our top priorities. Every drip and injection is administered by fully licensed, highly experienced registered nurses using only pharmacy-grade, FDA-approved ingredients to ensure your body receives exactly what it needs to thrive.
