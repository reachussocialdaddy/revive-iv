Questions?

Everything you need to know about our luxury IV hydration therapy.

What is IV Hydration Therapy?
IV hydration therapy is a fast and effective way to deliver necessary vitamins, minerals, and hydration directly into your bloodstream, completely bypassing the digestive system for 100% absorption.
Does the IV hurt?
Most clients feel only a slight pinch during the insertion of the IV. Our highly trained registered nurses are skilled at making the process as quick, painless, and comfortable as possible.
How long does a session take?
A typical IV therapy session lasts between 45 to 60 minutes, depending on the specific drip and your body's individual absorption rate. During this time, you can relax, read, or catch up on emails.
Are the treatments safe?
Yes! All our treatments are administered by licensed medical professionals using sterile, single-use equipment and FDA-approved, pharmacy-grade ingredients.
How quickly will I feel the effects?
Most clients report feeling a boost in energy and overall wellness immediately following their session, with the full benefits noticeable over the next 24 to 48 hours.
